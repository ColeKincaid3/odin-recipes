Ramen

Want to enjoy the best meal ever, have some top ramen

Its gods gift to those who dont have alot of money for food

Ingredients

  • top ramen package
  • microwave
  • instant ramen cooker

Cooking Instructions

  1. Open package and fill ramen cooker to line with water, then add in block of ramen
  2. Heat in the microwave for 5 minutes
  3. stir in seasoning and enjoy gods give to the unwealthy
